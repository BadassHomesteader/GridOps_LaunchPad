How to Log In

All GridOps modules use your Microsoft account — the same credentials you use for Outlook or Teams.

1

Click any module link above to open it in your browser (Chrome or Edge recommended).

2

You'll be redirected to the Microsoft sign-in page. Enter your organizational email and password.

3

If prompted to choose an account, select yours or click "Use another account".

4

That's it! Your session stays active so you won't need to sign in every time you visit.

Quick Tips

  • Bookmark your most-used modules for quick access.
  • One sign-in works across all GridOps modules.
  • Use the profile menu (top right) to view your account or sign out.
